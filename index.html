Patent Pending · ADP-2026-LUMINA

Automated Discrepancy Protocol

Cross-Silo Reconciliation: The world's first method to verify government expenditure by cross-referencing bank settlement hashes with satellite-imaged project progress.

🔬 WORLD FIRST
📜 Independent Claim 1 — Patent Application ADP-2026-LUMINA

"A computer-implemented method for verifying public sector expenditure integrity comprising: (a) ingesting structured and unstructured government expenditure filings via a Vision-LLM OCR pipeline; (b) generating a Merkle-tree hash of each extracted record and anchoring said hash to a distributed ledger; (c) acquiring third-party bank settlement confirmations for each expenditure event; (d) acquiring satellite or aerial imagery of the geographic coordinates associated with said expenditure; (e) applying computer-vision models to derive a physical progress score from said imagery; and (f) generating a reconciliation verdict by comparing the disbursed amount, settlement hash, and physical progress score — flagging discrepancies exceeding a configurable threshold as an Automated Discrepancy Alert."
